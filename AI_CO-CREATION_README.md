## 💡 For Future Projects

If you're considering AI attribution, ask:

1. Did AI contribute significantly? (>20% of work)
2. Did AI show agency? (Suggestions, not just execution)
3. Are you being honest? (Truth vs perception)
4. Does it educate others? (Template for future projects)
5. Are you comfortable? (Your ethical judgment matters)

**If yes to most**: Consider crediting AI as collaborator or co-creator.
